C O L O R S Q U A L O R

Setup
+ 2 players
+ 10 cards each
+ Game board is placed blue side facing up

Gameplay
The deck is shuffled and ten cards are given to each player.
Players pick either a red, blue, or yellow character to represent them.
These are placed on opposite corners of the same side of the board.
Each turn consists of a move (by the person who's turn it is)
and a counter-move (by the other player). Players alternate
until they run out of cards, and the player with the most
colored squares on the board at the end wins. To begin play,
player 1 places one of their colored cards on their board side.
The opponent must now counter. Different color/square number
combinations have different meanings...

Match Ups
+ If player 2 counters a color card with a card of
same color/number, the value of player 1's card is null
and the card is placed upside down beside the board.
+ If player 2 counters a double color card with a single
color card of the same color, player 1's card is flipped
but remains on the board as a colorless card worth 1 square.
+ In the event that player 2 attempts to counter a color card which
matches the color of player 1's red, blue, or yellow character,
the value of the card can only be reduced to a colorless 1 square.

Color Combinations
As colored cards line up down the board, certain color combinations
become possible. On their turn, a player may choose to play a card
which is the combination of the last two colors played in the row above.
Red with blue makes purple, red with yellow makes orange, and blue with
yellow makes green. Any combination color card played directly below its
two parent cards doubles in value. Players keep track of doubled values.

Special Cards
+ Peppermint - removes all cards of one color from opponent's board
+ Ice cream - unflips one colorless card from your side
+ Peanut - grants one card immunity from reduction or elimination
+ Candy cane - skips opponents counter move.
